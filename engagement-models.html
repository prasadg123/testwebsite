Build, Operate and Transfer

The Build, Operate and Transfer (BOT) model offered by Mhastech allows businesses to outsource their IT operations to reduce costs and improve efficiency. With the BOT model, Mhastech builds an offshore team based on the project needs and handles all administration, including payroll, accounting, and HR.

During the transfer stage, businesses can take over operative management and ownership of the project. The BOT 2.0 model offered by Mhastech allows businesses to save time and effort in building their teams, and since they are project owners from the start, there is no formal transfer process necessary.

Mhastech is committed to helping businesses reduce costs, maximize efficiency, and ensure reliable performance through the BOT model.
