Build, Operate and Transfer

At Mhastech, we offer a Build, Operate and Transfer (BOT) model that enables businesses to outsource their IT operations. Our BOT model is designed to help businesses reduce costs and improve their operations. We build an offshore team based on the project needs and stay focused on the product, taking care of payroll, accounting, HR, and the entire administration. The transfer stage is carried out when businesses decide to take over operative management. Our BOT 2.0 model allows businesses to save time and effort in building their teams, and since they are the project owners from day one, there is no need to do a formal transfer. We are committed to helping businesses reduce costs, maximize efficiency, and ensure reliable performance.
