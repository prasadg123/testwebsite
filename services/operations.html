Operations : Application Maintenance & Support

At MS, we understand that application support is essential to keep your business running smoothly. That's why we offer a comprehensive range of support services, including L1, L2, and L3 support, to meet your specific needs. Our experienced maintenance engineers work collaboratively with other teams such as development, infrastructure, network, Middleware, and DBAs to ensure that incidents and problem tickets are resolved efficiently. We utilize multiple ticketing tools, such as ServiceNow, Remedy, Jira, and Rally, to ensure that all of our clients' needs are addressed in a timely and organized manner. Our team is dedicated to providing the best possible support to ensure that your applications are always up and running smoothly, so you can focus on your business goals.

Our ITIL-certified support engineers have extensive experience conducting Disaster Recovery (DR) tests and initiating DR switches. This process gives you assurance in the event of a site failure and helps to establish the appropriate Recovery Time Objective (RTO) and Recovery Point Objective (RPO).
