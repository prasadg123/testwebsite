Application Management Services

Service Continuity

Our ITIL-certified support engineers have extensive experience conducting Disaster Recovery (DR) tests and initiating DR switches. This process gives you assurance in the event of a site failure and helps to establish the appropriate Recovery Time Objective (RTO) and Recovery Point Objective (RPO).

Operational Readiness

Our maintenance engineers are trained to perform operational readiness checks prior to production deployment. We ensure the implementation steps are practiced in a simulated environment, including the capability of returning to the previous stable release. Tasks such as log rotation, memory utilization, and the health of servers and applications are monitored on a regular basis.
